Our story

Why Hart Lending exists

Hart Lending began with a frustration shared by almost everyone who's ever taken out a loan: the broker who's everywhere during the chase, then nowhere once the deal is done. Advice wrapped in jargon. Recommendations you're expected to trust without ever being shown the reasoning.

We thought finance should feel different — especially when it's tied to the biggest decisions of your life. So we built a practice around three things the industry too often skips: showing our work, staying in touch, and telling the truth about how we're paid.

That's what the heart and the growing branch in our logo represent. The heart is care — we treat your money and your goals the way we'd treat our own family's. The branch is growth — because the right finance, structured well, helps your life grow over years, not just get you to settlement day.

Our promise is simple: you'll always see every option we considered, you'll never wonder what's happening, and we'll still be here long after the keys are in your hand.

What we stand for

Three values, no exceptions

Transparency

We show you every lender we looked at — and every one we didn't pick, and why. Our commission is disclosed plainly, never buried.

Communication

You'll never wonder what's happening. Hartbeat Updates™ keep you informed at every milestone — even when there's nothing new to report.

Partnership

We're not done when you settle. With your Post-Settlement Care Plan, we're your broker for life — at 30 days, 6 months, annually and every fixed-rate expiry.
